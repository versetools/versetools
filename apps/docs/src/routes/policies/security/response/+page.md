## We appreciate your concern

Keeping customer data safe and secure is a huge responsibility and a top priority. We work hard to protect our customers from the latest threats. Your input and feedback on our security is always appreciated.

## Reporting security problems

**Report security vulnerabilities** via [our bug report form](https://{config.domain}/bug-report). We’ll review your report and get back to you as soon as we can, usually within 72 hours.

**For other urgent or sensitive reports**, please email us at [{config.emails.security}](mailto:{config.emails.security}). We’ll respond as soon as we can.

**For requests that aren’t urgent or sensitive**: submit a support request at [{config.emails.support}](mailto:{config.emails.support}).
